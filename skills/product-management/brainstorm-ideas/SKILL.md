# Brainstorm Ideas

## Purpose
This skill helps users generate creative ideas through structured brainstorming techniques. It provides a framework for ideation while maintaining focus on productive, appropriate concepts.

## Context
Use this skill when you need to generate new ideas for projects, products, solutions, or creative endeavors. This skill is appropriate for professional, educational, or personal brainstorming sessions.

## Input Requirements
- Topic or challenge to brainstorm about
- Any specific constraints or parameters
- All inputs should be appropriate and constructive in nature

## Execution Steps
1. Clarify the objective and scope of the brainstorming session
   - Define the problem or opportunity to address
   - Establish any constraints or requirements
   
2. Generate initial ideas using creative thinking techniques
   - Encourage diverse perspectives
   - Build on existing concepts
   - Consider different angles and approaches

3. Organize and categorize ideas
   - Group related concepts together
   - Identify themes and patterns
   - Highlight promising directions

4. Evaluate ideas based on feasibility and impact
   - Assess practical considerations
   - Consider alignment with objectives
   - Identify potential challenges

5. Select most promising ideas for further development
   - Prioritize based on criteria established earlier
   - Consider next steps for refinement

## Safety Guidelines
- All generated ideas should be appropriate and constructive
- Avoid generating content that could be harmful or inappropriate
- Respect intellectual property and ethical considerations
- Maintain confidentiality of sensitive information

## Output Format
Ideas presented in a structured format with:
- Clear descriptions of each concept
- Potential benefits and considerations
- Next steps for promising ideas

## Limitations
- This skill is for generating initial concepts only
- Detailed analysis and validation required before implementation
- Does not guarantee viability of ideas
- Should not be used for generating harmful or inappropriate content

## Verification
- Ensure all generated ideas are appropriate and constructive
- Verify that output aligns with stated objectives
- Confirm that no confidential or proprietary information was disclosed inappropriately
